About

The purpose of this tool is to easily find your SERP Ranking - meaning what page number your domain falls under in the google search engine. I wrote this tool in hopes that it would help some people easily determine what page they are ranked on. As of right now it will only go 10 pages deep into the google results.

Two Easy Steps

  • Enter your domain name: please leave off http:// and https://
  • Keywords: this is the same thing as one of your vistors might type in the google search box
